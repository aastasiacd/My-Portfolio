About Me

Design is how I speak.

I'm Anastasia, a second-year Computing student at the University of Technology, Jamaica, and a self-taught graphic designer who's been building her craft one project at a time.

From promotional flyers and event banners for my club, to concept ads, editorial covers, and social graphics, I bring ideas to life with intention and style. I am currently a PRO for the UTECH Chess Club and served as Deputy Editor-In-Chief of the UTech Students Union Editorial Committee, designing real materials used by real people.

I'm detail-obsessed, fast-learning, and always up for a creative challenge.
